### Where does cross-field validation happen in Django?

Currently, Django's cross-field validation happens in `Form.clean()` - the form-level clean method. Individual field validators only receive the single field's value.

### Design Decisions

Looking at the Django forms architecture:
1. `Field.validators` - list of callables that take a single value
