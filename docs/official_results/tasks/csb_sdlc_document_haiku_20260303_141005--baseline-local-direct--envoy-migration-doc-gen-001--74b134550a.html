Frequently Asked Questions\n\n### Q: Do I need to change my configuration to upgrade to v1.31?\n**A:** No. v1.31 is backward compatible with v1.30 configurations. Observability mode works well for:\n- Monitoring and analytics (production-ready)\n- Passive observation (production-ready)\n\nIt may not be suitable if your external processor needs to reject or modify requests.\n\n### Q: How much latency does observability mode add?\n**A:** Minimal. Since responses are not awaited, latency is only from sending the message (~1-5ms depending on network).\n\n### Q: Can I enable observability mode for only certain routes?\n**A:** Not directly in filter config, but you can:\n1.
